PRIVACY POLICY

Privacy Policy

1. Introduction


MM Flowers is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use our website or services. By using our site, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect


We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide when you make a purchase or sign up for our newsletter. This includes your name, email address, phone number, and payment details. We also collect non-personal information such as your IP address, browser type, and browsing behavior to enhance your user experience and improve our services.

3. How We Use Your Information


The information we collect is used to process your orders, manage your account, and communicate with you. We may also use your information to send promotional offers, updates, and newsletters. If you prefer not to receive these communications, you can opt-out at any time by following the instructions in the email.

4. Sharing Your Information


MM Flowers does not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information with trusted partners who assist us in operating our website, conducting our business, or providing services to you. These partners are obligated to keep your information confidential and use it only for the purposes we specify.

5. Data Security


We implement various security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. Our website uses SSL encryption to ensure that your data is transmitted securely. We also conduct regular security audits and updates to maintain the highest standards of data protection.
