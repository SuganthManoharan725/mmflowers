FAQ'S

Frequently Asked Questions

1. What areas does MM Flowers deliver to in Chennai?


MM Flowers proudly serves the entire Chennai area, ensuring timely and fresh delivery to all major neighborhoods and business districts. Whether you're located in Koyambedu, Chinmaya Nagar, or beyond, our extensive delivery network guarantees that your floral gifts reach their destination in pristine condition. For more detailed delivery information, please contact our customer support team.

2. How do I place an order with MM Flowers?


Placing an order with MM Flowers is easy and convenient. You can order through our secure website, by calling our customer service team, or by visiting our store in person. Our online platform allows you to browse our wide selection of floral arrangements and gifts, making it simple to choose the perfect flowers for any occasion. If you need assistance, our friendly staff is here to help you every step of the way.

3. What types of floral arrangements are available?


At MM Flowers, we offer an extensive range of floral arrangements to suit every occasion. From elegant bouquets and sophisticated centerpieces to custom designs, our talented florists create stunning arrangements using the freshest flowers. Our selection includes traditional roses and lilies, exotic orchids, and vibrant tropical flowers, ensuring that you find the perfect arrangement for birthdays, weddings, anniversaries, and corporate events.

4. Can I customize a floral arrangement?


Yes, MM Flowers specializes in creating custom floral arrangements tailored to your specific preferences. Whether you have a particular design in mind or need guidance on selecting the right flowers, our experienced florists are here to bring your vision to life. Contact us to discuss your requirements, and we will craft a unique arrangement that exceeds your expectations.

5. What is MM Flowers' return and refund policy?


Customer satisfaction is our top priority at MM Flowers. If you're not completely happy with your purchase, please reach out to us within 12 hours of delivery. While we do not offer returns or exchanges, we are committed to resolving any issues you may have. For more details on our return and refund policy, please visit our website or reach out to our customer service team.
