About Us

Why Choose Us


MM Flowers: Your Trusted Florist Since 1950

MM Flowers has been a cornerstone in the flower industry since 1950. As a family-owned business spanning generations, we pride ourselves on delivering unparalleled quality and service from our prime locations in Koyambedu Flower Market and Chinmaya Nagar.

As a family-owned business spanning generations, we have maintained our commitment to excellence and personalized service. Our deep-rooted values and dedication to quality have helped us build a loyal customer base who trust us for their floral needs.


Exceptional Flower Quality in Chennai


At MM Flowers, we believe that the quality of our flowers speaks for itself. Sourced from the best local and international growers, our flowers are always fresh, vibrant, and long-lasting. Whether you need a bouquet for a special occasion or flowers for your home, we ensure that every bloom is perfect. Our attention to detail and commitment to quality make us the top choice for flower lovers in Chennai.



Wide Range of Floral Arrangements


We offer a wide range of floral arrangements to suit any occasion. From weddings and anniversaries to birthdays and corporate events, our talented florists can create stunning bouquets and centerpieces that will leave a lasting impression. Our extensive selection includes everything from traditional roses and lilies to exotic orchids and tropical flowers. No matter your preference, MM Flowers has the perfect arrangement for you.

Convenient Flower Delivery in Chennai


MM Flowers offers convenient flower delivery services throughout Chennai. Whether you're sending a gift to a loved one or decorating your own space, our reliable delivery service ensures your flowers arrive fresh and on time. We understand the importance of timely delivery, especially for special events, and strive to exceed your expectations with our prompt and professional service.

Personalized Service You Can Trust


At MM Flowers, we take pride in offering personalized service that caters to the unique needs of each customer. Our experienced staff is always ready to assist you with selecting the perfect flowers and creating custom arrangements that reflect your style and preferences. With decades of experience and a passion for floristry, we are dedicated to making your floral dreams come true.

Commitment to Sustainability


MM Flowers is committed to sustainable practices. We believe in preserving the environment while providing the highest quality flowers to our customers. Our eco-friendly approach includes sourcing flowers from sustainable farms, reducing waste, and using biodegradable packaging. By choosing MM Flowers, you're not only getting beautiful arrangements but also supporting a greener planet.

Community Engagement


As a local business, MM Flowers is deeply involved in the Chennai community. We support local events, charities, and initiatives that make our city a better place to live. Our community engagement reflects our belief in giving back and strengthening the bonds with our customers and neighbors. When you choose MM Flowers, you're not just a customer; you're part of our extended family.

Experience the MM Flowers difference by visiting our locations in Koyambedu Flower Market and Chinmaya Nagar. Discover the finest flowers in Chennai and let our skilled florists help you find the perfect blooms for any occasion. With our commitment to quality, exceptional service, and a wide variety of floral arrangements, MM Flowers is your go-to flower shop in Chennai.
